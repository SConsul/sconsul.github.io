Hello World

My name is Sarthak and I'm a senior undergraduate of IIT Bombay majoring in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science and Engineering. I intend to use this little corner of the internet to give an peek into my mind and share my work experience. To get an insight on my academic life so far, you can have a look at my CV or my Github profile.

I am interested in the statistical and mathematical aspects of perception, vision and image processing. I'm particularly interested in fields of Machine Learning and Deep Learning and applying them in the domains of medical imaging and robotics.

At present, I work with Prof. Amit Sethi at IIT Bombay on active learning approaches on medical data

In the summer of 2019, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. Ralph Müller at the Institute of Biomechanics at ETH Zurich on leveraging deep-learning to segment lacunae from 3D micro-CT scans of trabecular human bone tissue. In 2018, I was a student researcher at Medical Deep Learning and AI Lab - IIT Bombay on using deep-learning for the segmentation of nuclei from various 2D-medical datasets. I also worked on developing an user-facing Android application to screen lesions as a precursor to oral cancer.

I spend most of my time diving into books, videos and blogs across the internet looking for something new to learn and do. I am also fond of biking, reading fiction and photography.
